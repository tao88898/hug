Our Mission

Black Rights & Justice is a nonprofit-oriented platform dedicated to protecting the civil, legal, and human rights of Black individuals in the United States. We stand firmly against racism, racial violence, systemic discrimination, and hate-based harassment.

Our goal is to ensure that every Black person can live with dignity, safety, and equal opportunity — regardless of background, location, or socioeconomic status.

Fundamental Rights We Defend

  • The right to life, safety, and freedom from racial violence
  • Equal treatment under the law and by law enforcement
  • Protection against discrimination in employment and housing
  • Equal access to education and public services
  • Freedom of speech, protest, and civic participation
  • Protection from hate crimes and racial harassment
Reminder: Racial discrimination is illegal under U.S. federal and state law. No one should face abuse or unequal treatment because of their race or skin color.

If You Are Being Harassed or Abused

If you or someone you know is experiencing racial bullying, discrimination, or violence, consider the following steps:

  • Ensure your immediate safety and seek help if you are in danger
  • Document incidents (dates, locations, witnesses, evidence)
  • Report hate crimes or discrimination to local authorities when safe
  • Contact civil rights organizations or legal aid services
  • Reach out to trusted community leaders or advocacy groups

You are not alone. Speaking up helps protect both yourself and others.

Education & Public Awareness

Education is a key tool in dismantling systemic racism. We support public education on Black history, civil rights movements, and the ongoing challenges faced by Black communities in America.

  • Understanding systemic racism
  • Knowing your civil rights
  • How to be an ally against racism
  • Promoting equity and inclusion in schools and workplaces
